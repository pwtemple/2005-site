At PW Temple, we identify opportunities for re-use and restoration of buildings and development of sites with potential. A major strength lies in our policy of the complementary development of buildings on a single site location, making the most of any existing features.
We find solutions in keeping with the surroundings, whether they be isolated farmhouses and barns or within a community of size from hamlet to city.
PW Temple aim to achieve the seemingly contradictory qualities of being both unobtrusive and eye-catching - unobtrusive in that the building work should be entirely in harmony with the surrounding environment, and eye-catching in the quality of the workmanship and the imaginative use of appropriate materials.
We currently have a number of projects under construction or at the planning stage. We illustrate two of our developments here and have others which may meet your needs and capture your imagination.
