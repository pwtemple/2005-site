GALLERY

To the front of Pyerigg there are panoramic views across the Solway coast to Scotland, Sandale and round to the Caldbeck common, all from the the spacious garden with well kept lawn and mature borders.

.

There is a generous off road parking for a number of Vehicles .

 

For anyone wishing to walk, there are numerous footpaths in the area with access to the Uldale and Caldbeck Common within 5 minute walking distance
