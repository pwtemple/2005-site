Planning is now being sought to develop a range of redundant farm buildings in rural North Cumbria.
The project - based on the farmyard to a former monastery - includes five sandstone barns and a three-storey cornmill.
Again, a sympathetic development of the site is a prime consideration.
In view of the important historical connections of the site, PW Temple is working closely with Allerdale Borough Council's Conservation Department.
As with other developments undertaken by PW Temple, sale of the properties in advance will enable the purchasing clients to be involved in the decision-making for the internal design and layout.
All enquires about this rare opportunity to acquire a unique property in the area should be made direct to PW Temple.
