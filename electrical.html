Electrical Contracting
In 1995 our business expanded to provide an electrical contracting department, headed by a senior electrician with thirty years' experience.
Contracts carried out include general domestic wiring installations through to work in schools, office and churches, including Carlisle Cathedral.
  • Domestic and Industrial installations
    New installations, extensions to existing installations and full re-wire.
  • Security systems
    Intruder alarms, closed circuit television and door entry systems.
    Systems designed to suit client's individual requirements.
  • Fire alarms and emergency lighting
    From simple interlinked mains/battery smoke and heat detectors to complex addressable systems, with or without emergency lighting.
  • Periodic testing and inspection
    Existing installations are tested and reported. Applies to all categories of work including domestic, industrial and caravan site installation.
  • Portable appliance testing
    Any property open to the public - for example, holiday cottages, hotels and offices - should have their appliances tested.
We are NICEIC approved contractors and carry out all works strictly in accordance with their recommendations. We issue appropriate certificates on completion.
