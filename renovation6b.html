In 1999 we designed the renovation of a former 1960's property.
The property, a two-storey detached house, pebble-dashed and with tiled roof and aluminium windows, was entirely modernised to provide superb living accommodation, including the most up-to-date insulation methods and a central vacuum-cleaning system.
The new design incorporates natural stone under a slate roof, with wooden windows. Incredibly, the original structure was maintained in place within the magnificent exterior and it still exists - every single inch of floor space, on both ground level and first floor!
PW Temple worked closely with the clients, Mr & Mrs Lockhart, to achieve a development of true distinction. Not only did it win individual trade awards for plastering, stonework, plumbing and decoration, but it carried off the Highly Commended Award in the Allerdale Borough Council's "Built in Quality Award" and went on to win the coveted northern counties "Master Builder of the Year" competition run by the Federation of Master Builders.
