Renovations
If you have a property that you wish to upgrade, to restore or to renovate, PW Temple can build a complete solution.
We are pleased to be involved at the earliest stages to advise on potential. Working closely with clients, either in-house or in collaboration with leading architects and designers, we can progress ideas through to reality. If required, we will take a client through planning and submission of plans to the local authorities. For example, we have extensive experience and understanding of the regulatory framework for development in sensitive areas such as the National Park.
We pride ourselves on our care and attention to detail, including accurate estimation of costs so that we are able to deliver to our clients within agreed budgets.
Our project management skills mean that we can work efficiently and effectively and with the minimum of disruption to the client.
We use the best of modern techniques sympathetically. Our experience with traditional materials has won us work on buildings such as Carlisle Cathedral, and although we anticipate that a lattice arch may not be at the top of the priorities for the majority of our clients, we will always rise to challenges and offer appropriate solutions.
