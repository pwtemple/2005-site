Property Details:
Threshers Barn, Main Farm, Blindcrake

Three-storey barn with slate roof. Conversion of this unique property will include, on completion, a large garden (approx. 300 m2), private patio area (approx. 60 m2) and double garage.

Internally the property has on three floors the following:

  • Lower ground floor, approx. 70m2
    • Kitchen
    • Utility room
    • Dining room
    • Exit/entry doors lead from all three rooms to the patio

  • Ground floor, approx. 70m2
    • Living room with gallery above
    • Two bedrooms
    • Bathroom
    • Entrance hall

  • Lower ground floor, approx. 70m2
    • Two bedrooms
    • Bathroom
    • Access to the gallery

The property is afforded views towards Bassenthwaite and Skiddaw with the south elevation benefiting from early morning sunshine to the kitchen, dining, living and all bedrooms and the setting sun on the gable to the two window seats on the gallery.
Internal features will include exposed beams, a feature fireplace and hand-built oak kitchen - all creating a traditional feel to the property and retaining much character. The conversion also incorporates the most up-to-date technology: thermal insulation to walls, floors and ceilings; underfloor heating to give maximum efficiency; and the added luxury of a central vacuum-cleaning system.
The property has mains water, drainage, electricity and telephone and will have oil-fired central heating with timber double-glazed windows.
